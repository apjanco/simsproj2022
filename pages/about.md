## About Moving Stories Project

Moving Stories Project tracks Islamic manuscripts of the romance genre from and within India.

The project focuses on four works:

- Tutinama (link)
- Bahar-i Danish (link)
- Gulshan-i Ishq (link)
- Haft Sair-i Hatim (link)
